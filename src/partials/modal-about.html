HIGH RESULT AT THE INTERNATIONAL LEVEL

The main goal of our company has always been associated with the undeniable quality and food safety of its products. That is why, in order to achieve high results, we regularly undergo an audit for compliance with the requirements of international standards FSSC 22000(Food Safety System Certification 22000), IFS Food (International Featured Standard) and ISO 9001(Quality Management System) that guarantees quality and safety of our products to our consumers. “Our company confirms this great responsibility by the fact that almost 20 years ago, in 2004, we passed the first ISO 9001 certification, and in 2012, our company was the first among coffee bean producers to undergo a certification audit in accordance with the requirements of the 5 th version of IFS international standard (International Featured Standard Food). Today, according to version 6.1, this check for compliance with 400 criteria is a difficult test even for leading European companies,” Our CEO proudly tells about the achievements.
