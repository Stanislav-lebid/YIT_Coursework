  • Arabica

    This is the most common type of coffee, which is grown at higher altitudes. It has a more expressive taste and aroma, as well as less caffeine compared to other varieties.

    Arabica is characterized by a delicate and refined taste, with notes of fruits and flowers. Compared to other types of coffee, such as Robusta, Arabica has less acidity and less caffeine.

  • Robusta

    Robusta is characterized by a rougher, worse taste compared to the more delicate and floral taste of Arabica. It has more pronounced notes of dark chocolate and wood, and is thicker and fuller-bodied.

    One of the distinctive features of Robusta coffee is its higher caffeine content. Typically, Robusta coffee contains more than 2.2% caffeine.

  • Liberica

    Liberica coffee has a unique flavor that can be difficult to describe in conventional coffee terms. It often has aromas and flavors that differ from traditional coffee varieties.
